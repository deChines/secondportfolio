About Me

Hi, my name is Randy Chen and I'm an Interaction Designer based in Zürich. With my affinity for technology, I specialise on blending creativity with digital solutions. I'm passionate about creating meaningful experiences for the user by involving them in the process. This approach and interest in designing interactions and environments for the future inspired my work and has awarded me with the ZHdK Förderpreis and the Swiss Design Association (SDA) Bachelor Award.
